About Rosetta International.

Rosetta International is a British language company specialising in answering the different translation needs of the media including subtitling and dubbing from English into Arabic and vice versa. Executed from our offices in Amman, Jordan, we pride ourselves on meeting our international clients needs, from providing different language subtitles and dubbing to translation and copywriting…

Rosetta

Finding however, a great need to meet local translation needs as well as answering our own linguistic training requirements we opened Translation & Training House, TTH, a sister operation catering to local market clients in 2003. TTH is a unique and specialised centre of teaching and translation wherein Rosetta International’s new language recruits can be trained to meet the highest of standards. Catering for business as well as students from all walks of life, TTH is a dynamic institution of learning.
